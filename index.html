What to include

Be specific

Name the barrier, the adjustment requested, and the date by which a decision is needed.

Ask for reasons

If an organisation refuses, ask for written reasons and any alternative adjustment it proposes.

Keep evidence

Save a copy of the letter, replies, forms, booking references, screenshots, and dates of phone calls.
